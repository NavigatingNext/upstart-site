About Upstart

Upstart mentors students into high‑wage skilled trades through math prep, hands‑on learning, and real employer connections. Upstart is a program of Resilient, a faith‑based nonprofit mentoring youth into purpose and work. We are open to all.

Our values

  • Dignity in Work — Every student is created with purpose and potential.
  • Character & Safety — We cultivate habits and a safety‑first mindset.
  • Mastery Through Mentors — Growth via hands‑on coaching.
  • Earn While You Learn — Apprenticeships build skills and savings.
  • Service & Community — We build people who build our neighborhoods.
