Students

Pick the track that fits where you are today. All programs are free for participants.

Upstart: Explorations (Grades 6–8)

A one‑semester mentoring program meeting once a week. Laid‑back structure, homework help, and space to build confidence.

Upstart: Career Exploration (Grades 9–11)

  • 12 weeks of introductory math review, tutoring, and hands‑on STEM activities
  • Visits to trade sites and talks with professionals
  • Character development and life skills

Upstart: Career Readiness (Ages 18+)

  • Focused math prep to pass union apprenticeship exams
  • Resume building, interviewing skills, and workforce readiness
  • Scholarships to offset application/exam fees
  • Introductions to contractors—with work boots & tools provided for hires

How to apply

  1. Fill out the interest form on the Contact page.
  2. We’ll schedule a short info session with you and a parent/guardian.
  3. Complete placement activities so we can tailor support.
