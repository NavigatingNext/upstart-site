Common questions

Is it safe?

Students are trained in safety from day one. We use protective gear, structured supervision, and a safety‑first culture.

Will they have stability?

Trades face long‑term shortages. Apprenticeships lead to steady wages and clear advancement.

Does this limit college later?

No. Many students pursue further certifications or college after building skills and savings.

How is Upstart different?

Mentorship, small groups, employer partnerships, and faith‑rooted community support.
