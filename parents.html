College vs. Trades: What to Consider

  • Time to Earnings: Apprentices earn while they learn; many reach journeyman wages in ~4 years.
  • Cost: Trade school and apprenticeships typically cost far less than a four‑year degree.
  • Flexibility: Students can pursue college later with real‑world experience and savings.
